# Ch3 — Arrival on Arrakis

## Summary

House Atreides arrives on Arrakis. The desert is overwhelming — alien heat, optical distortions, no moisture, cinnamon spice smell in the air. Paul and Jessica land and travel to Arrakeen by groundcar. At a formal reception, they meet Stilgar (Fremen naib) and Dr. Liet-Kynes (Imperial Planetologist, secretly half-Fremen).

Stilgar's water-greeting (spitting on the table) introduces Fremen water culture. Kynes, seemingly neutral, is clearly running his own agenda. Duke Leto begins political work immediately — meeting with Fremen leaders.

Lady Jessica recognizes the Bene Gesserit's Missionaria Protectiva prophecies embedded in Fremen culture — and that she and Paul fit them perfectly. Paul's prescient dreams grow more vivid on Arrakis.

## Key Takeaways

- Arrakis is physically overwhelming; its reality hits harder than description prepared them for
- Fremen culture: water is sacred; the dead are processed for their water; eye color (blue-in-blue) marks spice saturation
- Sandworms (Shai-Hulud): enormous, sacred to Fremen, connected to spice cycle (mechanism unclear)
- Stillsuits: essential survival gear; Fremen-made suits decades ahead of off-world technology
- Jessica recognized Missionaria Protectiva — she and Paul fit the Fremen messiah prophecy
- Kynes has a secret terraforming project: making Arrakis green over centuries
