# Ch2 — The Household Prepares

## Summary

After Paul's test, Chapter 2 widens the lens to show House Atreides preparing to leave Caladan. Thufir Hawat (Mentat and Master of Assassins) briefs Paul on the political situation. Duke Leto appears for the first time — we see his command presence and pragmatic acceptance of a dangerous situation.

The chapter establishes the political landscape of the Imperium: the Great Houses (Landsraad), the Spacing Guild's dependence on melange, the CHOAM economic monopoly, and the Emperor's fear of Atreides. The offer of Arrakis is clearly a trap — but Leto accepts because the opportunity within the trap is real.

New characters introduced: Thufir Hawat (brilliant, ancient Mentat), Gurney Halleck (weapons master, scarred, musical), and Duncan Idaho (Swordmaster, already on Arrakis).

## Key Takeaways

- The Emperor Shaddam IV fears House Atreides' rising popularity → offered them Arrakis as a trap
- All melange (spice) comes only from Arrakis → whoever holds Arrakis holds civilizational power
- Thufir Hawat suspects a Harkonnen mole within Atreides ranks — identity unknown
- Duke Leto: pragmatic, beloved by his men, accepts risk for opportunity
- House Harkonnen ruled Arrakis for 80 years; they will have left saboteurs
