# The Voice

[[bene-gesserit]] technique: commanding other humans through precise modulation of tone, pitch, and cadence — near-irresistible to untrained minds.

## Definition

The Voice is a Bene Gesserit combat and influence technique. By modulating tone, cadence, and sub-harmonics of speech, a skilled practitioner can trigger involuntary compliance in another person. The target doesn't consciously choose to obey — their nervous system responds before the conscious mind can intervene.

## Effectiveness

Against untrained individuals, the Voice is effectively irresistible. A sufficiently skilled Bene Gesserit can stop a person in their tracks, make them drop a weapon, or send them running — with a single word.

Trained individuals can resist. Knowing what the Voice is and how it works provides partial immunity. Complete resistance requires significant practice.

## Paul's Training

[[lady-jessica]] secretly trained [[paul-atreides]] in Bene Gesserit techniques, including the Voice — despite him being male (the Bene Gesserit do not normally train men). As of Ch1, his proficiency is unclear, but the training has begun.

This is part of what makes him unusual and potentially dangerous in the [[bene-gesserit]]'s view: a male with Voice training and apparent prescience is outside their models.
