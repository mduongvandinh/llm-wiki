# Stillsuit

Full-body suit that recycles 99.7% of the wearer's moisture, enabling survival in the lethal desert of [[arrakis]].

## How It Works

A stillsuit covers the entire body. It captures moisture from sweat, exhaled breath, and urine, then filters and recycles it as drinkable water through a tube at the neck. A well-fitted stillsuit means a person loses almost nothing.

The Fremen-made suits are far superior to off-world designs. The gap is decades of technology — a result of generations of necessity-driven refinement.

## Cultural Significance

How well you wear a stillsuit is a cultural signal on Arrakis. The [[the-fremen]] can evaluate a stranger's seriousness about desert life at a glance:
- Properly fitted, no leaks = you understand Arrakis
- Loose, careless fitting = off-worlder who hasn't learned yet

When [[duke-leto-atreides]] ensures his household is properly fitted before landing, it signals respect. When someone's stillsuit leaks, it signals ignorance.

## Practical Importance

In a world with no open water, a stillsuit is survival. Without one, a person in the open desert loses lethal amounts of moisture within hours. With a proper Fremen-made stillsuit, a person can survive indefinitely.
