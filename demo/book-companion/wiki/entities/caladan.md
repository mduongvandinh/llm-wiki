# Caladan

The Atreides home world for 26 generations; oceanic, temperate, and green — everything Arrakis is not.

## Overview

Caladan is a water world — ocean-dominated, with temperate climate and abundant life. It has been [[house-atreides]]' home for 26 generations. The Atreides family castle sits above the sea.

In the story, Caladan functions as the "before" — the paradise that is being left behind. The contrast with [[arrakis]] is total: where Caladan is wet and green, Arrakis is parched and brown. Where Caladan is safe, Arrakis is lethal.

## Significance

The departure from Caladan carries enormous emotional weight for [[paul-atreides]], [[lady-jessica]], and [[duke-leto-atreides]]. Twenty-six generations of roots — abandoned, possibly forever.
