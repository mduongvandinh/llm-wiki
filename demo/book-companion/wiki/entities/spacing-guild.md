# The Spacing Guild

Controllers of interstellar travel; use [[melange]] to fold space; their complete dependence on spice makes them a silent stakeholder in Arrakis' fate.

## Overview

The Spacing Guild provides the only interstellar travel in the Imperium. Their Navigators use the spice melange to achieve a limited form of prescience — allowing them to safely navigate folded space. Without spice, there is no travel. Without travel, civilization collapses.

This makes the Guild uniquely powerful and uniquely vulnerable: they need spice absolutely, and spice only comes from [[arrakis]].

## Significance to the Plot

The Guild's existence explains why Arrakis is so important. Whoever controls the spice controls:
1. The Guild's supply → interstellar travel
2. CHOAM → economic power across the Imperium
3. The Bene Gesserit's [[kwisatz-haderach]] program (spice enhances mental abilities)

The Guild is a silent third party in the conflict over Arrakis. Their interests align with stable spice production — which may not align with whoever is trying to destroy House Atreides.
