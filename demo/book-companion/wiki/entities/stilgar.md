# Stilgar

Naib (leader) of a Fremen sietch; measured, authoritative, evaluated Paul and Jessica carefully at the reception in Ch3.

## Profile

- **Role:** Naib — leader of a Fremen desert community (sietch)
- **Faction:** [[the-fremen]]
- **Status:** Alive (through Ch. 3)
- **Notable:** Blue-in-blue eyes — no white visible; complete spice saturation from lifetime on Arrakis

## First Impression (Ch3)

Stilgar speaks little at the formal reception. He is watchful. He evaluates [[paul-atreides]] and [[lady-jessica]] with intense focus that others might miss. His manner is that of a man accustomed to making life-or-death assessments quickly.

He performs the Fremen water-greeting: spitting on the table as a gesture of respect. When Liet-Kynes explains this, Paul understands it correctly — offering your own body's water is the highest gift a Fremen can give.
