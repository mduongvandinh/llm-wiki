# Thufir Hawat

Mentat (human computer) and Master of Assassins for House Atreides; ancient, loyal, deeply worried about Arrakis.

## Profile

- **Role:** Mentat — Master of Assassins, chief intelligence officer for House Atreides
- **Faction:** [[house-atreides]]
- **Status:** Alive (through Ch. 2)

## What is a Mentat?

A Mentat is a human trained to function as a computer — pattern recognition, logic chains, probabilistic analysis — in a universe where thinking machines are forbidden (the Butlerian Jihad banned AI). Hawat is among the finest Mentats alive.

## Role in Ch2

Hawat briefs [[paul-atreides]] on the political realities of the Imperium. He is the one who makes explicit what Leto already knows: the offer of Arrakis is almost certainly a trap. His analysis:
- Emperor [[shaddam-iv]] fears House Atreides' rising popularity
- Arrakis is ungovernable: hostile desert, Fremen resistance, Harkonnen agents left behind
- Failure on Arrakis gives the Emperor grounds to destroy Atreides "legitimately"

Hawat also suspects there is a Harkonnen informant within House Atreides — a traitor. He has not identified who yet.
