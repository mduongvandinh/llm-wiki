# Arrakeen

The main city on Arrakis; former Harkonnen capital; now seat of House Atreides' rule on Dune.

## Overview

Arrakeen was [[house-harkonnen]]'s administrative center during their 80-year rule. [[house-atreides]] has taken possession of the governor's palace here.

## Architecture

Everything in Arrakeen is designed around water conservation:
- Windtraps on every building — devices that harvest atmospheric moisture
- Sealed construction to minimize evaporation
- People wear [[stillsuit]]s even in the city

The contrast with off-world cities is immediately obvious to anyone arriving from a water-rich planet.
