# House Harkonnen

Ancient enemies of House Atreides; former rulers of Arrakis for 80 years; almost certainly complicit in the plan to destroy Atreides.

## Overview

House Harkonnen is brutal by nature and reputation. Under their 80-year rule of [[arrakis]], they treated the [[the-fremen]] as subhuman — killing them casually, falsifying population counts. The Fremen hate them deeply, which is now an Atreides advantage.

They have been displaced from Arrakis but will not simply accept this. [[thufir-hawat]] is certain they have left behind saboteurs and agents within Arrakis — and possibly a mole inside House Atreides itself.

## Key Figures

- **Baron Vladimir Harkonnen** — mentioned with dread in Ch2; not yet appeared directly
- **Rabban** (the Beast Rabban) — Harkonnen governor on Arrakis; ruled through terror; mentioned in Ch3

## The Ancient Enmity

The hatred between Atreides and Harkonnen is both personal and political. The sources note it is old, deep, and mutual. No reconciliation is possible or expected.

## Current Threat

Even though they no longer hold Arrakis, the Harkonnen presence continues:
- Agents among the Arrakis population
- Possible traitor within Atreides ranks (Hawat suspects this)
- Likely working with the Emperor to ensure Atreides fails
