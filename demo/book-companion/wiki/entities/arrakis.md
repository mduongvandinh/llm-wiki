# Arrakis (Dune)

Desert planet; sole source of melange in the universe; new fief of House Atreides and the center of every major power struggle in the Imperium.

## Overview

Arrakis is the third planet of Canopus. It has no open water on its surface. The entire planetary surface is desert — sand, rock, and heat. The native people, the [[the-fremen]], have adapted over generations to survive here.

It is the only source of [[melange]] — which makes it the most important planet in the universe.

## Physical Environment

- No surface water; moisture is harvested from atmosphere via windtraps
- Heat extreme, even at dawn (felt through ship hull on arrival)
- Desert called the "bled" — vast, alien, visually overwhelming to newcomers
- Optical effects: the horizon looks wrong, dust changes perception of distance
- Cinnamon-like smell everywhere = trace spice in the atmosphere

## Arrakeen

The main city. Formerly [[house-harkonnen]]'s capital under the name Harko. Buildings designed to minimize moisture loss. Windtraps on every structure. The Atreides have taken over the governor's palace.

## The Sandworms (Shai-Hulud)

Enormous creatures living beneath the sand. They move toward rhythmic vibrations — footsteps, machinery, any regular rhythm. Contact means death. The Fremen have learned to move in irregular patterns to avoid attracting them.

The Fremen revere sandworms as sacred. Their connection to the spice cycle is mentioned but not yet fully explained as of Ch3.

## Strategic Importance

- All [[melange]] comes from Arrakis
- Melange → [[spacing-guild]] navigation → interstellar travel
- Melange → CHOAM economic control
- No spice = civilization collapses

This is why [[house-atreides]] being given Arrakis is potentially the greatest prize or the deadliest trap — possibly both simultaneously.
