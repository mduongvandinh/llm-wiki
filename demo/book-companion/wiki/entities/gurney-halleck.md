# Gurney Halleck

Weapons master for House Atreides; scarred, musical, fiercely loyal; trains Paul in combat.

## Profile

- **Role:** Weapons master, combat trainer
- **Faction:** [[house-atreides]]
- **Status:** Alive (through Ch. 3)
- **Notable:** Inkvine scar on jaw; plays the baliset (stringed instrument)

## Character

Gurney is a paradox — cheerful and musical, yet one of the most dangerous fighters in the Imperium. He trains Paul with intensity but warmth. The scar on his jaw is from inkvine, a Harkonnen torture weapon; he has personal reasons to hate House Harkonnen.

His bond with Paul is like an older brother or uncle — teasing, demanding, protective.
