# House Atreides

Noble house of the Landsraad; rising in power and popularity in the Imperium; now controlling Arrakis under circumstances that look like a political trap.

## Overview

House Atreides has held [[caladan]] for 26 generations. They are known for the unusual loyalty they inspire in their people — not feudal obligation, but genuine devotion. This is partly what makes them threatening to Emperor [[shaddam-iv]]: a beloved, militarily strong noble house is a potential rival.

The Emperor's offer of [[arrakis]] — the most valuable fief in the Imperium — is almost certainly designed to destroy them. [[thufir-hawat]] has analyzed this clearly. Duke [[duke-leto-atreides]] accepted anyway.

## Current Situation

House Atreides has relocated to Arrakis. They face:
1. A political trap whose exact form is unknown
2. Harkonnen agents left behind among the population and possibly within Atreides ranks
3. A hostile environment (the desert itself is lethal)
4. An opportunity: the Fremen as potential allies, the spice wealth as leverage
