# Reverend Mother Gaius Helen Mohiam

The Emperor's Truthsayer; ancient, severe Bene Gesserit who administered the Gom Jabbar test to Paul — and was shaken by the result.

## Profile

- **Full name:** Reverend Mother Gaius Helen Mohiam
- **Faction:** [[bene-gesserit]]
- **Role:** Emperor's Truthsayer; high-ranking Bene Gesserit authority
- **Status:** Alive (through Ch. 1; not present Ch. 2-3)

## Personality & Motivations

Mohiam projects cold, complete authority. She is ancient and has administered this test many times. Yet Paul unsettles her — he endured with unusual calm, then demonstrated Bene Gesserit-level perception by reading *her* fear. She finishes the test with more concern than she expected to feel.

She is also [[lady-jessica]]'s former teacher and superior. Part of her visit to Caladan was surely to assess what Jessica's defiance produced.

## The Gom Jabbar Test

Mohiam administered the [[gom-jabbar]] to Paul in Ch1:
- Held a poisoned needle (the gom jabbar itself) to his neck
- Made him place his hand in a pain-induction box
- Test lasted several minutes
- Paul endured through mental discipline, not flinching
- Her assessment: he is human. But also: he is "dangerous" and possibly the [[kwisatz-haderach]] — "one generation early."
