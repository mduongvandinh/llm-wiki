# Dr. Liet-Kynes

Imperial Planetologist assigned to Arrakis; secretly half-Fremen and their judge/prophet; harbors a decades-long dream of transforming Arrakis into a green world.

## Profile

- **Full name:** Dr. Liet-Kynes
- **Role:** Imperial Planetologist (officially); Fremen judge and prophet (secretly)
- **Faction:** Nominally the Emperor's; spiritually [[the-fremen]]
- **Status:** Alive (through Ch. 3)

## Dual Identity

Kynes is the Emperor's man on paper — the Planetologist responsible for studying Arrakis' ecology. But his father, Pardot Kynes, was the first Imperial Planetologist and became deeply embedded in Fremen culture. Liet grew up among the Fremen and is accepted as one of them.

He functions as their judge and prophet — a position of immense hidden authority. The Fremen trust him deeply. This makes his official neutrality a performance.

## The Green Dream

Kynes has inherited and continued his father's secret project: terraforming Arrakis over centuries. The Fremen are planting deep-root vegetation in hidden places, slowly shifting the water cycle. The goal: a green Arrakis, centuries from now. This project is unknown to the Emperor or the off-world powers.

*This puts Kynes' interests in direct tension with the spice trade — a fully terraformed Arrakis might disrupt the spice ecology.*
