# Duke Leto Atreides

Head of House Atreides; tall, hawk-faced military leader who accepts a dangerous political trap because he sees the opportunity within it.

## Profile

- **Full name:** Duke Leto Atreides
- **Age:** ~40s (estimated)
- **Faction:** [[house-atreides]]
- **Role:** Duke, head of household, Paul's father
- **Status:** Alive (through Ch. 3)

## Personality & Motivations

Leto is pragmatic above all. He knows — openly — that the Emperor's offer of Arrakis is almost certainly a trap. Thufir Hawat has warned him. He accepts anyway, because the spice wealth and the chance to forge an alliance with the Fremen represent an opportunity no Duke could pass up. This is calculated risk, not naivety.

He commands intense loyalty. His men follow him not from obligation but genuine devotion — a recurring theme in how others describe House Atreides. This loyalty is one reason the Emperor fears him.

He loves Jessica and Paul deeply, though political constraints prevent him from formalizing the relationship with Jessica.

## Key Relationships

- [[paul-atreides]]: His son and heir. He trusts Paul's training and judgment.
- [[lady-jessica]]: His concubine; the relationship is genuine and warm despite the formal title.
- [[thufir-hawat]]: Trusted Mentat; Leto listens to his strategic assessments carefully.
- [[gurney-halleck]]: Weapons master; loyal and close.

## Key Moments

- **Chapter 2:** First appearance. Physically imposing — hawk-faced, military bearing. Delivers the now-iconic quote about the sleeper awakening.
- **Chapter 3:** Immediately begins political work on Arrakis — meeting with Fremen leaders (including Stilgar), trying to build trust faster than the Harkonnen trap can close.

## Notable Quotes

> "Without change, something sleeps inside us, and seldom awakens. The sleeper must awaken." — Ch2
