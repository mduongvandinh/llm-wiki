# Dune — Chapter 1: The Gom Jabbar

## Summary
Paul Atreides, aged 15, is awakened early morning by the Reverend Mother Gaius Helen Mohiam — the Emperor's Truthsayer and a high-ranking Bene Gesserit. She has come to test Paul, a test she claims all humans must eventually face.

## Characters Introduced

**Paul Atreides**
- 15 years old, son of Duke Leto Atreides and Lady Jessica
- Has been trained by his mother in Bene Gesserit ways, despite being male (unusual)
- Recurring prescient dreams about a Fremen girl on a desert planet
- Shows unusual calm under extreme pressure during the test

**Lady Jessica**
- Paul's mother, Duke Leto's concubine (not wife — political reasons)
- A Bene Gesserit who defied orders: was told to bear only daughters, bore a son instead
- Clearly loves Paul deeply; her anxiety during the test is visible to him
- Has trained Paul secretly in the Weirding Way (Bene Gesserit techniques)

**Reverend Mother Gaius Helen Mohiam**
- The Emperor's Truthsayer
- High-ranking Bene Gesserit
- Ancient, severe, commands total authority
- Tests Paul with the gom jabbar — a poisoned needle at his neck + pain box (box induces intense pain in hand)
- Tells Paul: "I hold at your neck a gom jabbar. The gom jabbar — the high-handed enemy. It kills only animals."
- Observes Paul with intense focus; seems surprised and impressed by his performance

## Key Concepts

**The Gom Jabbar Test**
- Purpose: distinguishes humans from animals. A "human" can overcome instinct with reason.
- Method: subject places hand in box causing intense simulated pain. Needle at neck = death if hand is withdrawn.
- The test lasts several minutes; Paul endures it through mental discipline
- "You've heard of animals chewing off a leg to escape a trap? There's an animal kind of trick."

**Bene Gesserit**
- Ancient sisterhood, political manipulators operating across the Imperium
- Possess "the Voice" — ability to command others through tone and cadence
- Running a multi-generational breeding program (Kwisatz Haderach project)
- Jessica is a Bene Gesserit; she trained Paul in their techniques

**Kwisatz Haderach**
- Bene Gesserit term for a male who could be what a Reverend Mother cannot be
- The goal of their centuries-long breeding program
- Mohiam hints Paul could be this... but "one generation early" — a danger

## Key Quotes

## Locations Mentioned
- **Caladan**: The Atreides home planet. Described as wet, oceanic, green. Peaceful.
- **Arrakis (Dune)**: Referenced only — the desert planet they will be moving to. Paul's dreams are set there.

## Foreshadowing
- Paul's dreams of a Fremen girl on Arrakis (introduced but not named yet)
- Mohiam's concern: Paul is "dangerous" — more powerful than expected, but born too early
- Jessica's guilt about defying Bene Gesserit orders — she loves Leto and wanted a son
