# Dune — Chapter 2: Preparing for Arrakis

## Summary
After the test, Paul wanders the castle and reflects. We learn more about House Atreides preparing to leave Caladan for Arrakis. Paul receives a lesson from Thufir Hawat (Mentat) about politics. Duke Leto appears — we get our first impression of Paul's father.

## Characters Introduced / Developed

**Duke Leto Atreides**
- Paul's father, head of House Atreides
- Tall, hawk-faced, moves with military precision
- Clearly loves Paul and Jessica despite political constraints (Jessica is concubine, not wife)
- Pragmatic leader: knows the move to Arrakis is a trap but sees opportunity within it
- Quote: "Without change, something sleeps inside us, and seldom awakens. The sleeper must awaken."
- Commands intense loyalty from his men

**Thufir Hawat**
- Mentat (human computer) — Master of Assassins for House Atreides
- Ancient, loyal, brilliant strategist
- Trains Paul in the political realities of the Imperium
- Deeply concerned about the move to Arrakis; suspects a trap

**Gurney Halleck**
- Weapons master for House Atreides
- Scarred face (inkvine scar on jaw), plays baliset (stringed instrument)
- Trains Paul in combat; cheerful, musical, fierce fighter
- Close bond with Paul; like an older brother/uncle figure

**Duncan Idaho**
- Swordmaster of the Ginaz (elite fighter, rare title)
- Has already gone to Arrakis as advance scout
- Paul admires him greatly; referenced but not present in this chapter

## Key Political Context

**The Great Houses (Landsraad)**
- Noble houses that collectively balance the Emperor's power
- House Atreides is rising in popularity and military strength — this threatens the Emperor

**CHOAM**
- The Combine Honnete Ober Advancer Mercantiles
- Controls economic power across the Imperium
- Whoever controls Arrakis controls spice → controls CHOAM → controls the Imperium

**The Spacing Guild**
- Navigators who use spice to fold space for interstellar travel
- Completely dependent on melange (the spice)
- No spice = no space travel = civilization collapses

**Melange (The Spice)**
- Only found on Arrakis
- Extends life, enhances mental ability, enables Guild navigation
- Most valuable substance in the universe
- Addictive; withdrawal is fatal

## The Political Trap
Thufir explains to Paul: Emperor Shaddam IV is giving House Atreides the fief of Arrakis (previously held by House Harkonnen). This is likely a trap:
- Arrakis is nearly ungovernable: harsh desert, hostile Fremen population, Harkonnen saboteurs left behind
- If Atreides fails → Emperor can destroy them without direct conflict
- But Leto accepts: the spice wealth and military opportunities outweigh the risk

## Key Locations

**Caladan**
- Atreides home world for 26 generations
- Oceanic, temperate, rich
- About to be left behind — emotional weight for the family

**Arrakis (Dune)**
- Desert planet, no open water on surface
- Produces all of the universe's melange
- Populated by Fremen (native people, fierce survivors)
- Harkonnen had controlled it for 80 years before being displaced

## Factions Update

**House Harkonnen**
- Previous rulers of Arrakis
- Ancient enemies of House Atreides (personal and political hatred)
- Led by Baron Vladimir Harkonnen (not yet appeared, but referenced with dread)
- Will have left behind agents and sabotage on Arrakis

**The Emperor (Shaddam IV)**
- Fears House Atreides' growing power and popularity
- Likely behind the plan to destroy them using Arrakis as the weapon

## Open Questions

- Who is the Harkonnen agent within House Atreides? (Thufir suspects there is one)
