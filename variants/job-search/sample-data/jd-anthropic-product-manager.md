# Anthropic — Product Manager, Claude

## Job Description

Anthropic is hiring a Product Manager to work on Claude — our AI assistant. You'll work directly with research, engineering, and policy teams to shape what Claude can do and how people use it.

## Responsibilities
- Define product strategy and roadmap for Claude capabilities
- Work closely with research teams to translate model improvements into user-facing features
- Analyze usage data, user research, and feedback to identify highest-impact improvements
- Coordinate across safety, policy, and engineering to ship responsibly
- Write clear product specs and requirements; lead prioritization discussions

## Requirements

**Required:**
- 4+ years of product management experience
- Strong analytical skills: comfortable with data, A/B testing, metrics
- Excellent written communication: PMs at Anthropic write a lot
- Genuine curiosity about AI; deep interest in where this technology is going
- Experience working with ML/AI teams or on AI-powered products

**Nice to Have:**
- Background in AI/ML (even academic)
- Experience at a research-focused organization
- Prior experience managing safety or trust & safety products

## What Anthropic Offers
- Competitive salary + equity
- Remote-first culture
- Mission-driven work: "responsible development of AI for humanity's long-term benefit"
- Small teams, high ownership
- Deep access to frontier AI research

## Company Context
- ~500 employees (2026), growing fast
- Series E (~$7.3B total raised)
- Partners: Google, Amazon (major investors)
- Revenue: Claude API + Claude Pro/Team subscriptions
