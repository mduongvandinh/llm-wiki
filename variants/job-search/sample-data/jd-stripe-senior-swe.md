# Stripe — Senior Software Engineer, Payments Infrastructure

## Job Description

Stripe is looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join our Payments Infrastructure team. You'll work on the systems that process billions of dollars in payments globally — reliability, latency, and correctness are our highest priorities.

## Responsibilities
- Design and build highly reliable distributed systems that process payments at scale
- Own critical infrastructure from design through production — you'll be on-call
- Lead technical projects and mentor junior engineers
- Collaborate with product, security, and ML teams to ship new payment features
- Contribute to our engineering culture through code reviews, design docs, and RFCs

## Requirements

**Required:**
- 5+ years of software engineering experience
- Strong fundamentals: distributed systems, data structures, algorithms
- Experience with high-availability systems (99.99%+ uptime requirements)
- Strong communication skills — you'll write a lot of design docs
- Proficiency in one of: Ruby, Go, Java, or Scala (Stripe's primary languages)

**Nice to Have:**
- Experience with payments, fintech, or financial systems
- Background in database internals or storage systems
- Experience with on-call and incident management at scale

## What Stripe Offers
- Competitive salary + meaningful equity
- Remote-friendly (most teams are hybrid; some fully remote)
- Generous PTO, healthcare, 401k
- Learning budget ($1,000/year)
- Home office stipend

## Company Context
- ~10,000 employees (2026)
- Series I / Pre-IPO stage
- Processing hundreds of billions in payments annually
- Engineering-forward culture: high bar for code quality, strong emphasis on writing
